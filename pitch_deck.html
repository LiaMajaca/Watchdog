Slide 6: Core Innovation

Human-in-the-Loop (HIL): Self-Improving Security

“Our core innovation in Khusela ensures the system *learns* from new fraud patterns as fast as they emerge.”

1.

Analyst Override

When an analyst confirms a case is legitimate despite a high risk score (false positive), this decision is immediately logged.

2.

Feedback Loop Integration

The human decision is fed back into the Khusela intelligence engine, turning human judgment into machine training data.

3.

Model Retraining Simulation

Simulates immediate model adjustment, closing the gap between machine prediction and human reality to stay ahead of evolving fraud.
